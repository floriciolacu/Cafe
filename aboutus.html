A hug in a cup.

No depresso. Positivitea.

Our cafe is unique in our country. The concept of "A hug in a cup" started when we observed how much a cup of coffee or tea can help.
As our motto says, "No depresso. Positivitea.", we want to offer people that happiness of tasting a good drink, that gets you started to a better day, with more energy and good feeling.

The story

7.04.2016
First day

The first day was the hardest of the all. We just opened up and we didn't know what will happen, if people will come or no.
Before we opened, we tried to make ourselves known, but we still didn't know what to expect.
First clients were our friends who were supporting us. Then it was silence.
After a while, people started to show up. Not so many, but it was great for us seeing that.

How we increased

People were coming, but not so many and the bussiness didn't go as well as we expected.
We were quite disappointed, but we couldn't give up on our dream. So we started to promote our cafe even more using social media, flyers, giving special offers to people.
This way, we managed to grow our cafe and get more clients.

Now

Now our bussiness is growing day by day and we are very happy and proud.
We want to offer our clients best drinks and for them to leave happy and come again.
We also plan on opening more coffee shops in many locations.
